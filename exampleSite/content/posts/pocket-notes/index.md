This post turns the older pocket-notes folder into a complete demo bundle so it can participate in homepage cards, archives, and related content.

The image is intentionally prominent because the theme works best when covers carry part of the browsing experience. On a phone, that means faster scanning and less visual fatigue between entries.

A short body is enough here. The point is to make the list page feel alive while keeping the content structure simple and reusable.
